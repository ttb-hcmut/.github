# Bach Khoa presentation templates for Quarkdown

Unofficial templates for writing presentation in Bach Khoa (HCMUT) University. We are not affiliated with Bach Khoa in any way.

# A word of caution

Quarkdown isn't a general-purpose typesetting language like TeX or Typst; it does seem to specialize in HTML-based slideshows and the language for scripting is limited and for arguably good reasons at that. Quarkdown's presentations boast their own opinionated style.

Due to this, while we try to be faithful to the Bach Khoa style as much as possible, it's unlikely to 100% replicate slides written in TeX and Typst. We cannot guarantee that this template will be approved for thesis and capstone et cetera. Use this at your own risk!
